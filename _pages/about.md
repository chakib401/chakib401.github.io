I am an industrial PhD student (CIFRE) at the Centre Borelli at Université Paris Cité, and Informatique Caisse des Dépôts, both located in Paris. I am broadly interested in unsupervised learning. In particular, I have done work on :
- attributed graphs,
- representation learning,
- clustering, 
- co-clustering & biclustering, 
- multi-view clustering, 
- discrete optimal transport,

among others. In addition to research, I do some data science work as well as some web development.
